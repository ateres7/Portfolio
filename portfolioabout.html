This was my submission for OCAD's Face2Face postcard exhibition, which happened during my orientation in first year. It's a self portrait, done in Adobe Illustrator and edited with filters afterwards, referenced from a photograph.

So, a little bit about me. My name is Ha-Neul Kim, although I go by Helen. I am Korean-Canadian, so I was born in South Korea, but I grew up in Canada. I speak both English and Korean, though I am far more fluent and comfortable in English, as I use it more often. My language and culture is very important to me, and I utilize both in my artworks often.

I currently attend OCAD University, majoring in Graphic Design, although I'm taking the chance to explore more forms of art while I'm in school and have the time and resources. I enjoy experimenting with many forms of art, such as sketching, painting, bookbinding, and various forms of digital art. Given the chance, I will likely continue to try out new disciplines, and explore more forms and styles of art.
